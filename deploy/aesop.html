Aesop's Fables

— Typography, 2020 / Adobe Illustrator

  • Objective —

    To create a fold (accordion fold or gatefold) that depicts a chosen story from Aesop’s Fables using interesting typography

  • About —

    The Fox and the Goat is a story of a fox stuck in a well and a thirsty goat. The fox urges the goat to jump into the well and drink the fresh water, and then tricks him into helping the fox climb out, promising to help the goat get out in return. As soon as the fox makes it out of the well, he runs off, leaving the goat stuck. Moral of the story: “Look where you leap.”

  • Process —

    Special emphasis was placed on the progress of the interaction between the Fox and the Goat. The “title page” contains a little hint giving away the locations of the Fox and the Goat in the beginning and the end of the story.
