Home Kitchen Supplies

— Web Design, 2020 — Sketch, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript

  • Objective —

    To design a website with five web pages with certain requirements such as implementing Google Maps, Instagram posts, carousel, zoom features, form, and basic motion graphics

  • About —

    Home Kitchen Supplies is a hypothetical company that sells kitchen supplies that are appropriate for the home kitchen. The company has three offices (one headquarters and two branches) and both online and brick-and-mortar stores.

  • Process —

    The process consisted of including a more saturated set of colours, carefully placing the text for each section, and allowing for breathing space. Images were selected and placed on the site with careful consideration for how they fit the brand image. All photos are from Unsplash.
