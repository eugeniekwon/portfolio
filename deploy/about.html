hello! my name is eugenie / jaeeun.

I'm a recent Design Intern at Prophet (NY) and a graduate from Drexel University with a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Design and minors in Marketing and Music. I am open to exploring different disciplines of design although I am particularly interested in learning more about brand identity, package design, and typography. During the design process, I use storytelling and problem-solving in various ways to create engaging outcomes. As a long-term goal, I hope to create purposeful and thoughtful designs that make an impact.

In my spare time, I enjoy doodling, cooking, singing, taking photos, and getting coffee! I also prefer to eat cereal at night, and I have perfect pitch.

If you’d like to connect, feel free to send me an email — I’m a night owl, so there’s a chance you’ll receive a reply from me at 2 am, but either way I’ll get back to you soon!
