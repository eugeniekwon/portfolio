Philadelphia Flower Show

— Website Redesign, 2020 / Sketch, HTML5, CSS3

  • Objective —

    To redesign a website of an existing event

  • About —

    The Philadelphia Flower Show is an event held annually in Philadelphia, with a different theme each year. It showcases a variety of floral arts and collections and offers various hands-on opportunities and activities for visitors.

  • Process —

    The process consisted of including a lot of breathing space, choosing colours and typefaces that fit the event, finding and adjusting the tone of the images, and utilising the grid system effectively.
