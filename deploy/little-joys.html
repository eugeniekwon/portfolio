Little Joys in Life

— Independent Project, 2020

  • Objective —

    To create a self-driven collection of photographs with a chosen topic, method, and subject

  • About —

    We all might be busy living our own lives but we can still find little joys throughout. Little Joys in Life showcases a collection of photographs that depict people in their most natural state of pursuing what they love regardless of how great in size it may be: from getting flowers to playing music.

  • Process —

    People in these photographs were interviewed prior to their photoshoots to grasp a better understanding of what they are like. All photographs were required to be in black-and-white.
