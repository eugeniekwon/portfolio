Hidden Gems of Philadelphia

— Street Photography, 2019-2020 / Adobe Photoshop, Bridge

  • Objective —

    To capture people, buildings, and/or objects for street photography

  • About —

    The name Philadelphia comes from the word “phileo,” which means “brotherly love.” Streets of Philadelphia are captured alongside people who live there.

  • Process —

    Walking to a variety of places within Philly was one of the biggest factors in being able to create a diverse series of photographs. Keeping a sharp eye on the interesting activities happening throughout the city was also crucial. All photographs were required to be in black and white.
