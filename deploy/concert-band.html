Drexel University Concert Band

— Brand Identity, 2020 / Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

  • Objective —

    To create a design that can represent the band and bring more awarness to it To strengthen the connection of current student musicians and alumni to the band through merchandise

  • About —

    Drexel University Concert Band provides the opportunity for students to pursue and/or continue playing music in their college career. The group consists of students in various majors and disciplines and different instrument groups such as woodwinds, brass, and percussion. Upon joining the group, all students are required to audition. Concert Band has twice-a-week rehearsals with a concert at the end of each term (fall, winter, and spring) showcasing a variety of repertoires, which is open for admissions to the general public.

  • Process —

    The brainstorming began with generating music-related components, which soon turned into a deducted form. The final logo focuses on a variety of meanings and interpretations shown in an abstract direction.
