Copenhagen

— Illustration, 2019 / Adobe Illustrator

  • Objective —

    To choose a travel destination and create a travel poster, postcard, and stamps

  • About —

    Copenhagen, the capital city of Denmark, is well-known for its hygge lifestyle, which has helped it become a popular destination for travellers.

  • Process —

    The process consisted of combining three elements: Nyhavn Harbor, fish (fishing), and mountains. A combination of reference photos were used in order to create this composition.
