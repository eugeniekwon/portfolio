Woodmere Art Museum

— Brand Identity, 2020 / Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

  • Objective —

    To create three types of logos: pictorial, letterform, and wordmark To create applications for them (business cards, etc.)

  • About —

    Woodmere Art Museum focuses on showcasing artwork of local artists in Philadelphia and connecting the arts with nature.

  • Process —

    For the pictorial logo, the concept of frames and acorns were combined in order to reflect the mission of displaying art created by local artists and the museum’s location in the middle of nature. These three colours were chosen to best portray the look and feel of nature.
