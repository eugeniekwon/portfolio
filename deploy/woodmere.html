Woodmere Art Museum

— Brand Identity, 2020

  • Objective —

    To create three types of logos: pictoral, letterform, and wordmark To create applications for them (business cards, etc)

  • About —

    Woodmere Art Museum focuses on showcasing artwork of local artists in Philadelphia and connecting the arts with nature.

  • Process —

    For the pictoral logo, the concept of frames and acorns are combined in order to fulfill the purpose of displaying art created by local artists and the location being in the middle of nature. These three colours were chosen to best portray what nature looks and feels like. The key point of this project was simplicity and professionalism.
