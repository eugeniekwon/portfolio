Luis Barragán

— Poster Design, 2019 / Adobe Illustrator

  • Objective —

    To create a poster for a lecture series of an architect To include information on the speaker’s name, date, and location

  • About —

    Luis Barragán was born and raised in Mexico. He considered himself a civil engineer and architect. After finishing his education, he travelled to Europe in order to practice his own studies, which is when he was drawn to landscape architecture. He later focused on creating apartments, residents, and architecture with a focus on landscape. He drew much of his inspiration from the culture and religion of Mexico.

  • Process —

    Out of the many architectural works that Barragán created, one of his most famous, Casa Gilardi, was a big influence in terms of creating the structure. The long hexagons were influenced by his sculpture Torres de Satélite. The colours reflect his style. Futura was chosen as the typeface due to its symmetrical structure.
