Perused

— Publication Design, 2020 / Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Sketch

  • Objective —

    To create a magazine publication, masthead, covers for current and upcoming issues, and pages for a table of contents, an opening spread, and additional spreads

  • About —

    Perused is a lifestyle magazine focused on publishing informative content on current trends that can be read with attention or skimmed through. Areas of focus include arts and culture, crafts, travel, home and interiors, cooking, and more, with content that is both national and international. The target audience is college students and young adults who are interested in expanding their knowledge and trying out new lifestyle trends.

  • Process —

    A series of image making and typographic studies were practiced in the creation of this publication. For this publication, special emphasis was placed on the scale of images and the typographic experimentation as well as on having an organized layout. All images in the magazine are from Unsplash and have been modified.

Peruse (v.)

1. : to read with detail

2. : to skim through text
