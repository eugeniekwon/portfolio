Ocean Dumping

— Typography, 2019 / Adobe Illustrator

  • Objective —

    To create posters for four lecture series on the topic of ocean dumping with different speakers To emphasize unity and uniqueness across all of the posters

  • About —

    Ocean dumping is one of the many environmental issues facing our planet today that continue to become more and more urgent. According to statistics, about 6.4 million tonnes of plastic are dumped into the ocean each year.

  • Process —

    Emphasis was placed on the floating typography of “ocean dumping.”
