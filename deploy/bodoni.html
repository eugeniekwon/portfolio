Bodoni

— Typography, 2020 / Adobe Illustrator

  • Objective —

    To design a poster representing the typeface Bodoni, influenced by its history and creation

  • About —

    Bodoni was created by Giambattista Bodoni in 1798, and was influenced by John Baskerville. During the Neoclassical period, a lot of designs were focused on decorative, elegant aspects as well as on expressing life through art. Bodoni was initially considered a “classic” design, but later came to be seen as “modern” due to the rise in printing. Bodoni is well-known for its dynamic stroke difference between the thick and the thin, which was created with mathematical precision.

  • Process —

    In the early stages, no references to Bodoni’s historical background were applied. Soon after the research phase, a series of motifs and ideas were merged where numerical values, alphabets, and symbols were incorporated in order to reflect the typeface’s history.
