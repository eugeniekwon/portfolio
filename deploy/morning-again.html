Morning Again

— Package Design + Brand Identity, 2021 / Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

  • Objective —

    To create a rigid construction box including three to four items that shows branding

  • About —

    Morning:Again is a brand that creates small, compact kits that have items that help consumers to experience a “good morning” especially away from home. It includes a toothpaste, toothbrush, facial wash, and vitamin c gummies.

  • Process —

    The naming behind Morning:Again occurred with a shortened phrase of “good morning” to simply “‘morning!”. Because mornings come again each day, the “again” was followed after. The colons imitate the alarm clock that ring in the morning. There may be people who are extreme morning people or extreme non-morning people, or somewhere in between. Or some people had to change their morning schedules according to their lifestyle or work circumstances. Because there are different emotions associated to it depending on the preference, the three emoji faces show as the main motif. The alarm clocks represent the typical morning times that people wake up, and the suns also add to the motifs. Regardless of someone being a morning person or not, this kit is created to help people to have a good morning with the items inside. This kit is usually meant to be used when a consumer has to wake up in a different environment other than home: away on a camping trip, working late night to early morning shifts for frontline workers, students going through an all nighter at studio, and so forth. In terms of the package overall, the exterior has more of a subtle background with vibrant motifs and typography; however, as soon as the package is opened, a welcoming bright orange is shown with fierce copywriting that is created to keep someone awake and/or to at least give a small smile in the morning.
